Privacy Policy

WSQSI.com is committed to protecting your privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. It is part of our Terms of Use agreement. It does not cover the use of information or practices of any person or entity beyond WSQSI.com's control.

What Information We Collect
When you register to become a member, we collect your name, mailing address and email address and other information that you submit to the site. You have to be a registered member to visit WSQSI.com's web pages. Credit card number and other sensitive information are collected through private phone calls. We limit access to personal information about you to employees who we believe reasonably need to come into contact with that information to provide products or services to you or in order to do their jobs.

Cookies
WSQSI.com use cookies for only one place to remember your FPW username for your convenience while signing in. And, we do not write even one more extra byte of any other contents to your PC hard disk during your use of FPW, except what your browser needs to view, which is saved to your temporary internet files folder by your browser program. WSQSI.com automatically records information, including your IP address and the page you request and its history, on our server side.

How WSQSI.com Protects Your Information
WSQSI takes your security seriously and takes reasonable steps to protect your information. We use industry standard security protocols to protect your information.

How WSQSI.com uses Your Information
We use your information only for the business of WSQSI.com. WSQSI does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or with nonaffiliated companies. They will not be made available to any other members. We will not share your personal data with a third party.  In the event that another company buys the assets or stock of WSQSI.com, your personal information may then belong to another entity.

Reporting incidents

  • If you believe your WSQSI account has been compromised, please report the incident using our feedback form.

  • If you have been contacted by someone about your WSQSI account asking for a password, or other personal information, please forward the email to us. Please include the full headers and the HTML source code of the email you received.

  • In the event that you believe that your personal safety is at risk or if you believe that you may be the victim of identity theft or other illegal conduct, please contact the appropriate federal, state or local law enforcement agencies directly.
